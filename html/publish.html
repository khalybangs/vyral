User Author

Being a User Author gives you the ability to upload both books and article/journal contents at a given subscription fee, assigned by the author.

Any given user account can upgrade their accounts to a User Author account for free but will be charged 5% per user subscription.

Functionalities

- Upload Books: Authors can upload books with functions such as the ability to characterise and number chapters, glossary and information section, cover, title & contents respectivelly

- Create Articles: Artices that has a structure of the basic Journal structure and contents can be uploaded as well with the advantage of categorization and access fee

- Promotions: Just like any other Journal post and account, an Author can also promote their contents and profiles to reach a higher audience.

- Unlimited access: An author can change their subscription fees and can turn on or turn on their Author functionalities at any given time.
